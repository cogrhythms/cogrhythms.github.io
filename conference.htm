Rhythmic Dynamics and Cognition

June 4-5, 2013

View Poster Here

The Rhythmic Dynamics and Cognition Conference is a two-day event sponsored by the Cognitive Rhythms Collaborative (CRC). The program will be held at the Brain Building (Building 46) on the MIT campus (Room 3002) and will include lectures, a reception, and a poster session. Registration for each day is free but required by May 20, and is done through crc_conference.eventbrite.com. Lunches will be provided.

Out of town guests should make their own reservations in Boston (click here for hotels in the area). Please note that the conference dates are close to MIT graduation, so it is important to book early.

If you are interested in presenting a poster, please register at crc_conference.eventbrite.com by May 1 and send your title and abstract to xiaoshi@bu.edu.
